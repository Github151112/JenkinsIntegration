Assertion:

Normally we are doing validation with the help of decision making statement
but in testng we are having one feature
i.e: Assertion

We are having 2 types of Assertion:
1.Hard Assert
2.Soft Assert

Hard Assert:
1.In Hard Assert we are having static methods.
2.if hard assert get failed then it will terminate the execution
3.in hard assert we are not having any compulsory method.

Soft Assert:
1.in soft Assert we are having non-static methods.
2.if softassert will get failed it will not terminate the execution.
3.in softAssert we are having one compulsory method
assertAll();
